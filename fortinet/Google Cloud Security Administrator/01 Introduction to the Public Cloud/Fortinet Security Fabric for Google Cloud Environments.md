Fortinet secures Google Cloud environments by delivering a **Security Fabric** designed for the cloud, which focuses on: **Broad Protection**, **Native Integration**, and **Management & Automation**.

A variety of Fortinet products are available on the Google Cloud Marketplace to support different security needs:

- **Network and Secure Connectivity:** FortiGate (for Next-Generation Firewall and VPN/SD-WAN).
- **Application Security:** FortiWeb and FortiWeb Cloud (WAF solutions).
- **Visibility, Analysis, and Management:** FortiManager, FortiAnalyzer, and FortiCASB.
- **Advanced Threat Defense:** FortiSandbox.

This approach allows Fortinet solutions to natively integrate with Google Cloud services, providing unified visibility, control, and consistent policy management across hybrid and multi-cloud deployments.
